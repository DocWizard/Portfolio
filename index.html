About Me

Hi! I am Maciej, an aspiring tech writer. With seven years of dedicated experience in the realm of public television, I am driven by an insatiable appetite for fresh challenges. My tenure in this dynamic field has honed my adeptness at performing exceptionally under immense time pressure, viewing deadlines as catalysts that energize my creative prowess.

In addition to my journalism background, I've ventured into the exciting domain of artificial intelligence. While I may not claim expertise, I find joy in experimenting with local language models and image generators. It's a fascinating journey of discovery, where every encounter with AI opens up new avenues of understanding and creativity.

Beyond the pixels and paragraphs, I'm a tech enthusiast at heart. Exploring the intersection of storytelling and technology has become my passion, and I am eager to share this journey with you through my portfolio.
